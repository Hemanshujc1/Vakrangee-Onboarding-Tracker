## Phase 1:  HR admins portal

# Roles: 
 HR_super_Admin --> (add/delete new employee, track and verfiy the onboarding forms, assign the employee to Department), assign new employee to Hr_Admin (see all the employee under hr_Admin and able to see the onboarding process of the employee)
 
 HR_Admin --> See Onboarding of the new employees assign to him by the Hr-super_admin (add/delete new employee, track and verfiy the onboarding forms, assign the employee to Department)
 
HR_super_Admin add the employee and send the email with the login details of the portal to the new employee and assign the hr_admin to the employee which will do the onbaording procees of that employee --> Employee login on the portal  --> Employee fill the Basic Details --> Employee fill the prejoining forms --> The hr will then verify the details --> HR will open the post joining forms on the day of joining (once the employee reach the office) --> Employee now needs to fill the post joining forms --> Hr will verify it and status of the employee changes to (Active Employee).
